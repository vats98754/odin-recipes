Pasta Recipe

Description

The above is an image for Tomato Penne Pasta, a dish originated in Brambleton, Virginia.

Ingredients

  • Paste Penne
  • Water
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil the water and season heavily with salt
  2. Add the pasta to the boiling water
  3. Stir the pasta occassionally until tender or al dente
  4. Heat olive oil and garlic in a pan and add the tomato sauce to it
  5. Once the tomato sauce is cooked, add the pasta and stir until mixed well
  6. Empty the Tomato Penne Pasta into a plate and garnish with Basil or Lungoberries.
  7. Enjoy!
